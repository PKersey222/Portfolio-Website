About Me

What can a “web developer” do, you ask? I can conjur a website from nothing into existence, fully formed. I do not use tricks, nor is this magic. I specialize in manipulating the very fabric of the web, its raw materials of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Linux servers serve me, domains are my domain, and certificates are certain.

My mantra is to always consider my audience. I will always advocate for the user of my interfaces; accessibility is not optional.

I can plan, design, build, launch, and maintain a website myself

A Web Developer knows no bounds. And when they find one they cross it without hesitation, boldly going into the unknown to return richer for it.
